Internship at CEAS

CEAS Internship Programme

CEAS Internship Programme is highly competitive, particularly beneficial to students and younger researchers from diverse backgrounds. Interns will get an opportunity to develop various skill sets during the programme. They will be mentored by senior scholars and paired with other interns for collaborative research. A certificate will be provided on satisfactory completion of the internship.

Eligibility Criteria

Open to students pursuing Undergraduate, Masterâ€™s, or PhD programs. Selection is purely merit-based. Candidates with a strong spirit to learn and grow will be given high priority.

Internship Duration

The internship is flexible, with a minimum commitment of one month.

Application Procedure

Send an email expressing interest to contact.ceas@christuniversity.in with the documents listed below. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview.

Documents to be Submitted

1. Curriculum Vitae (CV)
2. Two letters of recommendation from present or past institutions
3. An original writing sample (published or unpublished work) of around 2,000 words
4. Statement of Purpose
